Almond Cream Cake

Description

This is a very delicious recipe for almond cake with whipped chocolate cream.

Ingredients

For the cake

  • 3 eggs
  • 200g almond flour
  • 200g sugar

For the topping

  • 200g cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 100g grated chocolate

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 160°C. Grease a springform pan.
  2. For the cake dough, separate the egg whites from the yolks. Beat the whites until very stiff.
  3. Carefully add sugar and almond flour to the egg whites, mix only as much as necessary to just get everything mixed together.
  4. Put the dough into the pan and bake at 160°C for around 30 minutes.
  5. For the topping, whip the cream. Add vanilla sugar and grated chocolate.
  6. When the cake is cold, put the whipped chocolate cream on top.
